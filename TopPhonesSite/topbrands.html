Top of Phones

Main Top Brands List of Phones

Apple

Apple is an American corporation, manufacturer of personal and tablet computers, audio players, smartphones, software and digital content. One of the pioneers in the field of personal computers and modern multitasking operating systems with a graphical interface. The headquarters is in Cupertino, California. Thanks to innovative technologies and aesthetic design, Apple has created a unique reputation in the consumer electronics industry, comparable to a cult.

Samsung

South Korean group of companies, one of the largest chaebols, founded in 1938. It is known on the world market as a manufacturer of high-tech components, including full-cycle production of integrated circuits, telecommunications equipment, household appliances, audio and video devices. The company's main office is located in Seoul.

Huawei

A Chinese company, one of the world's largest telecommunications companies. Founded in 1987 by former engineer of the People's Liberation Army of China Ren Zhengfei.

Xiomi

A Chinese corporation founded by Lei Jun in 2010. In 2021, it became the leader in smartphone production. It also produces household appliances, tablets, smart watches, software, electric scooters, electric bicycles and much more.

OnePlus

A Chinese smartphone manufacturing company for the global market. It is a division of BBK Electronics.
